Section Example

Section is a generic container for content that is not a semantically distinct section of the document. It is typically used to divide up a page into sections that are logically related, but not semantically different enough to warrant their own section element.

Article Example

Article is a self-contained composition in a document, page, application, or site and that is, in principle, independently distributable or reusable, e.g. in syndication. This could be a forum post, a magazine or newspaper article, a blog entry, an object, or any other independent item of content.
